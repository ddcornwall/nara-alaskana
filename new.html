National Archives Alaskana Explorer 0.9: Newly Cataloged Records

At the current time (January 2018), this application searches for items in the National Archives from the Alaska Digitization Project and displays the ten most recently cataloged records.

Please be patient as it may take up to one minute for records to appear. This is a known issue and being worked on.

This is a personal project of Daniel Cornwall, a librarian/coder living in Alaska. He can be contacted through his blog's about page.
